About

Hi there.

I'm Lindsay, an aspiring developer, marketing dropout and collector of driver's licenses from all over the US. I'm originally from Chicago and I currently live in Washington with my partner, Doug, and our ferocious beast of a pup, Daphne.

I have spent the past year (inadvertently) knocking out some of life's most significant changes — a cross-country move, home purchase, marriage and a career change. While it was a tumultuous couple of months, I am thrilled that life has led me to the incredible city of Seattle. I am so proud to be a student at the Ada Developers Academy, and I can't wait to transition into the world of software engineering.
