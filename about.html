Addison M. Cole

She, Her || They, Them

Thank you for your interest in wanting to know more about me! I am a 32 year old programmer from Memphis, Tennessee.It has been a passion of my work and personal life to acknowledge and validate the intersections of individual identities. I dream of changing the many interlocking systems of oppression which disproportionately affect queer, trans and people of color, and develop systems which are more equitable and compassionate. In my free time I am usually at home in Everett spending quiet time in my tiny house with my partner Dana and the most dapper Norwegian forest cat, Dharma. I can also often be found hunting for vinyl records, playing soccer, solving bouldering problems or, queering your local D&D tables with drag-queen vampire bards and gay luchadores. I have also been known to play guitar and banjo (and occasionally harmonica) in bands with friends.
