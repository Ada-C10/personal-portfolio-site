About Me

Currently a student at Ada Developers Academy, my short-term goal is to become a full stack developer. Inspired by natural themes and modern trends, I enjoy creating aesthetically appealing simple designs. I also have a growing interest in the movement and use of data. My long-term goal is to unite my interests in technology with my cliche but genuine hopes of making the world a better place.

Born and raised in Seattle, I have been fortunate enough to have been surrounded by beautiful mountains, rocky beaches and blue skies my whole life. A graduate of the University of Washington in Molecular Biology, I changed my career path to explore a field that is always suprising me with new innovations. In my free time I enjoy crafting, cooking, beach combing and 'ohhing and ahhing' over fuzzy little creatures.
